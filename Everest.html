Everest Base Camp

Overview

Maximum Elevation: 5600 meters, Kala Patthar

Duration:14 days

Dificulty:Moderate to hard

Accomodation:Teahouse

Permit needed:TIMS, Sagarmatha National Park Permit

The Everest Base Camp Trek is one of the beautiful and most trekked routes in Nepal. Many tourists visit Nepal to walk in the Sagarmatha region to enjoy the enticing mountain views. The trek starts from Lukla- a flight from Kathmandu.
